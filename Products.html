Proucts and Events

We buy and sell second-hand costumes(price list) Thursday
of every month we also hold a “CossiePlay Party” at the town
hall. Parties start at 6pm and run until 11pm. There is always a
live DJ at the party (who takes requests).

Cost

Entry is $15 for adults, $10 for ages 6-17
and kids under 6 are free. Each entry includes
one non-alcoholic drink and a glow stick.
